ACCEPTED INVENTORY

What We're Looking For

We buy pairs with a market. Here's what we accept:

  • Nike / Jordan Brand (all silhouettes)
  • Adidas (Yeezy, Ultraboost, Samba, NMD)
  • New Balance (990 series, 550, 2002R)
  • Asics (Gel-Kayano, Gel-Lyte, collaborations)
  • Rare and limited-run collaborations (any brand)
  • Deadstock and used pairs in good-to-excellent condition

Counterfeits, heavily worn pairs, or shoes without a verifiable market value will not be accepted. When in doubt, submit — we'll let you know.
