Built by Shoeheads, for Shoeheads

Custom Kicks started because the sneaker market needed a better option. Too many marketplaces with too many fakes, too many vague condition descriptions, and too much friction between a buyer and a pair they could actually trust.

We built Custom Kicks to fix that — a marketplace where every pair is authenticated before it ever ships, where sellers get fair offers without the runaround, and where the standard is set by the people who actually collect.

Our Mission

The Standard for Authenticated Resale

Custom Kicks exists to make the sneaker resale market trustworthy — for buyers who want confidence, and for sellers who deserve respect.

OUR STANDARD

We Don't Sell Fakes. Period.

Authenticity isn't a marketing line for us — it's the entire business. Every pair that comes through Custom Kicks is examined before it goes up for sale.

Our authentication process checks construction, stitching, tags, box details, and sole markings against known authentic references. Pairs that don't pass don't get listed. It's that simple.
