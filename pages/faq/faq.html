Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about buying, selling, shipping, and authenticity — all in one place. If you don't find your answer here, we're one message away.

Buying on Custom Kicks

Are your sneakers actually authenticated? +

Every pair on Custom Kicks is inspected and authenticated before it's listed. We check construction, materials, labeling, and box details against known authentic references. If it doesn't pass, it doesn't get listed — full stop.

What's your return policy? +

We stand behind every listing. If a pair arrives materially different from how it was described, contact us within 48 hours of delivery — we issue a full refund or replacement. All sales are final on accurately described pairs.

How does shipping work? +

Once your order is confirmed, we ship within [NEEDS CLIENT REVIEW: processing time, e.g., 1–3 business days]. You'll receive a tracking number by email. Pairs are packed to protect them in transit.

What payment methods do you accept? +

We accept all major credit cards, PayPal, and Apple Pay.

What condition are the shoes in? +

Every listing includes a specific condition description — Deadstock, New with Box, Used — Excellent, or Used — Good. We describe condition honestly. If a pair has a scuff on the toe or a crease in the box, we'll say so.

How do I track my order? +

You'll receive a shipping confirmation email with a tracking link as soon as your order ships. Use that link to follow your pair door-to-door.

Selling to Custom Kicks

How do I submit a pair? +

Head to our Sell page, fill out the submission form with the basics — brand, model, size, condition, and your asking price — and hit submit. Our team reviews every submission and follows up directly.

How is the offer price determined? +

We price based on current resale market data for that specific model, colorway, and size. We don't lowball — our offers reflect what the market actually pays.

How long does the process take? +

We review submissions and follow up within [NEEDS CLIENT REVIEW: turnaround time, e.g., 24–48 hours]. Once you accept an offer and ship the pair, payment is released after we receive and authenticate it.

How do I get paid? +

Payment is sent to you directly once your pair clears our final inspection. We'll confirm the method when we send the offer.

Do you accept used pairs? +

Yes — condition matters, but used pairs in good-to-excellent condition are welcome. We authenticate used pairs the same way we authenticate new ones. Be honest in your submission description and we'll be straight with you on the offer.
